Diverse Loan Programs

Conventional Loans

Flexible financing for various home purchase and refinance needs.

  • Competitive fixed and adjustable rates.
  • Options for Primary, Second, or Investment properties.
  • Minimum 3% down payment for qualified first-time buyers.
  • Various terms available (10, 15, 20, 25, and 30 years).

FHA Loans

Government-backed loans designed to make homeownership affordable.

  • Low down payment starting at only 3.5%.
  • More lenient credit score requirements (minimum 580).
  • Higher Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratios allowed.
  • Down payment can be gifted from family members.

Easy Jumbo Loans

Simplified high-balance financing for luxury real estate.

  • Loan amounts exceeding standard conforming limits.
  • Flexible asset and income verification options.
  • Available for both Purchase and Cash-Out Refinance.
  • Streamlined underwriting for high-net-worth individuals.

Apartment Loans

Specialized lending solutions for multi-family property investors.

  • Financing for 5+ unit multi-family properties.
  • Non-recourse and DSCR-based lending options.
  • Prepayment penalty structures tailored to your goal.
  • Available for acquisitions and portfolio refinancing.
